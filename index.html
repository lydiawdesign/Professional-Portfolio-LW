ABOUT LYDIA

Hi! My name is Lydia Williamson. I am an Website and Product Data Manager from Cincinnati, Ohio. I am the daughter of two architects. I graduated from the University of Dayton in December of 2017 with a degree in Communication with a concentration in Media Production and 2 minors - one in Visual Arts and the other in Business Administration.

Since I graduated, I’ve spent the last two years living abroad in Morocco. I’ve learned many things while living abroad like how to work with a diverse group of people, how to be resilient and strong, and how to market myself. My whole life I’ve been passionate about traveling, creating things, and art.

My first ever job was when I was 15 and I was a lifeguard. I’ve come a long way since then with many different odd jobs and titles. From being a nanny to a receptionist, a waiter, a bartender, a barista, an art teacher, a mural painter, a graphic designer, Creative and Content Strategist, Marketing Coordinator, to my most recent title of Website and Product Data Manager at a Promotional Product Supplier. Each job has taught me a new set of skills and has helped me narrow my field and refine myself professionally.

I thrive somewhere inbetween analytical and creative. I’m a creator and a curator by nature; I pride myself on my strong work ethic, my ability to find solutions, and my unique viewpoint. I am a passionate person who loves to learn and try new things. If I don’t know how to do something, I’m always the first one who wants to learn. I work well in a team environment (and find myself to be a leader typically) but I don’t mind taking on solo projects head-on.
